Types of Water Sports

Water Board Sports

Wakeboarding

Wakeboarding is a Board sport where person is pulled by a boat or cable while they are strapped on a board.

Wakeskating

Wakeskating is like wakeboarding but the rider is not strapped to the board.

Wakesurfing

Wakesurfing is done at much slower speeds and you dont hold on to a rope and you ride the tall wake thats produced right behind the boat.

Kneeboarding

The rider is strapped to a board while in the kneeling position.
